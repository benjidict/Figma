FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What happens if my item doesn't arrive? +
If your item doesn't arrive within the agreed time, TrustNest will initiate a full refund from escrow. Simply open a dispute from your Orders page and our team will investigate within 24–48 hours.
How long does escrow hold the payment? +
Funds are held in escrow until you confirm delivery, or for a maximum of 7 days after the seller marks the order as delivered. After 7 days with no dispute, funds are automatically released.
How do I become a Verified Seller? +
Register as a seller, then submit a valid government-issued ID through your Seller Dashboard. Our admin team reviews submissions within 1–3 business days and grants your Verified badge upon approval.
What is the evidence upload for? +
Uploading an unboxing video or photos protects you as a buyer. If the item arrives damaged or not as described, this evidence is used by our dispute team to decide on a refund or fund release.
Is TrustNest free to use? +
Creating a buyer account is completely free. Sellers pay a small platform fee only upon successful transactions. There are no listing fees or subscription costs.
What payment methods are accepted? +
TrustNest currently accepts GCash, Maya (PayMaya), and major Philippine bank transfers (BDO, BPI, UnionBank, Metrobank). More payment options are coming soon.
